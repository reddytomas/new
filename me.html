Background

I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, and lived there until I was 7 years old. I made my way back to Buenos Aires for my last year of high school, and recently in 2019 I have once again made it my home.

When I was 7 years old, my family and I moved to St. Louis, Missouri. I later studied at The University of Missouri in Columbia, Missouri where I received my undergraduate degree in Marketing. I later lived and worked in Chicago, Illinois after graduating from college.

The majority of my teenage years were spent in Quito, Ecuador, where I lived from 2006-2011.
