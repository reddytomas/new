About Me

I got my start working for Marketing agencies doing all sorts of entry level jobs and titles, until I realized that I'm way more interested in the creative part of the process. I went from working as an "Account Representative" and even doing sales to taking a design course and quickly discovering that UX Design is where I felt at home.

However, working in the client side of Marketing and doing sales has helped me to learn to work with people better, and understand how to navigate through the needs of clients, in order to truly satisfy their needs, which is at the essence of UX.

Now, I'm interested in helping brands reach the needs of their customers in a more impactful and direct method, through the art of creating.
